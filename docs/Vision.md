# AI Product Copilot

## Vision

AI Product Copilot is an AI-powered workspace that enables Product Managers to move from product discovery to product delivery faster by automating repetitive work and providing AI-assisted decision making.

Instead of using multiple disconnected tools, Product Managers can manage PRDs, customer feedback, meetings, feature prioritization, analytics, and product documentation in one intelligent workspace.

Our goal is not to replace Product Managers but to amplify their effectiveness by acting as an AI teammate throughout the product lifecycle.

## Mission

Help Product Managers save 10+ hours every week while improving product quality, communication, and decision making through responsible AI.

## Product Principles

1. AI assists, never replaces human decisions.

2. Every AI response should be explainable.

3. Every AI output should be editable.

4. Every AI action should be measurable.

5. Everything should belong to a Project.

6. AI should remember project context.

7. Professional quality over flashy features.

## Problem Statement

Product Managers spend significant time writing documentation, analyzing customer feedback, creating Jira tickets, summarizing meetings, prioritizing features, and preparing stakeholder updates.

Existing AI tools solve isolated problems and lack shared context, forcing users to switch between multiple applications.

This fragmentation reduces productivity and creates inconsistent product documentation.

AI Product Copilot provides a unified workspace where AI understands the entire project and assists throughout the product development lifecycle.
